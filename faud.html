Food App UX Design

OVERVIEW

The Montreal Food Delivery ChatBot App is Restaurant located in the suburb of a metropolitan area. The Restaurant strives to deliver a healthy, specialty dishes. The Restaurant target customers and workers who lack time or ability to prepare a family dinner

ROLE

UX Designer, User Research, Interaction, Visual Design, Prototyping and Testing from conception to delivery

GOAL

The goal of this particular project was to design a an app for Montreal that allows users to easily order and pick up fresh healthy meal

DURATION

April 2021 – December 2021

RESEARCH STUDY DETAILS

I conduced interviews and created empathy map to understand the users I am designing for and their needs. A primary user group identified through the research was working adults who don’t have time to cook. This research also revels the time was not the only factor limiting users from cooking at home. Other users problems includes Obligations, interest or challenges that makes it difficult to get groceries fro cooking or go to restaurant in person

PERSONA

The problem statement of one of the user , Anne is a nurse with a large heart who is stressed and overworked. But she is still motivated by her ability to help take care of her patients and fight against illness.



USER JOURNEY MAP

The journey map reveals how helpful it would be to access a dedicated Restaurant Food Delivery App.



KEY CHALLENGES

IA

TIME

ACCESSBILITY
